του άρθρου 5 της Συμφωνίας, εγκρίνονται με κοινή πρά−
ξη των αρμόδιων κατά περίπτωση Υπουργών.

όργανα (Π.Δ. 63 − ΦΕΚ 98/Α/22.4.2005).

του υπό έγκριση Πρωτοκόλλου δεν προκαλείται πρό−

σικές διατάξεις της Συμφωνίας και που είχε εκτιμηθεί
στην ΄Έκθεση του Γενικού Λογιστηρίου του Κράτους.

αποφασίζουμε:

PROTOCOL OF THE 3rd SESSION OF THE JOINT
INTERGOVERNMENTAL COMMITTEE ON ECONOMIC, INDUSTRIAL
AND TECHNOLOGICAL COOPERATION BETWEEN
THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC AND THE REPUBLIC OF AZERBAIJAN
(Baku, February 2, 2011)

The 3rd Session of the Joint Intergovernmental Committee on Economic,
Industrial and Technological Cooperation between the Hellenic Republic and the
Republic of Azerbaijan was held in Baku, on February 2, 2011, in accordance with
the provisions of the Agreement on Economic, Industrial and Technological
Cooperation, signed between the two countries in Baku, on July 26, 1994.
Deputy Minister for Foreign Affairs, Spyros Kouvelis, headed the Hellenic
Delegation.
Minister of Industry and Energy, Natig Aliyev, headed the Azerbaijani
Delegation.
The lists of the Participants of both delegations of the Session are attached to
this Protocol, as Annexes I and II respectively.
1. ADOPTION OF THE AGENDA
The draft Agenda was adopted and is attached to this Protocol as Annex III.
2. INTRODUCTORY REMARKS
During the meeting of the Joint Intergovernmental Committee on Economic,
Industrial and Technological Cooperation, which was conducted in a friendly
atmosphere of mutual understanding, both Sides, presented an overview of current
developments in their economies and reviewed their bilateral economic and
commercial relations, since the 2nd Session of the Committee, held in Athens, on
July 5-6, 2007. Both Sides stated their readiness to take relevant measures, in order
to improve their bilateral relations, on the basis of mutual interest.
The following conclusions resume the works of this Session:
3. BILATERAL ECONOMIC AND TRADE COOPERATION
Both Sides exchanged information regarding economic policy, which may
enhance and strengthen the development of bilateral economic and trade
cooperation.

In the Customs field, the Agreement between the Government of the Hellenic

held in Baku, on June 22, 2004, in the framework of the state visit of the then

in Athens, on February 22, 2009, in the framework of the state visit of H.E Ilham

Baku, on May 12, 2009, in the framework of the visit of the then Deputy Minister
for Foreign Affairs. In the margin of that forum, a Memorandum of Understanding
(MoU) between the Hellenic Federation of Enterprises and Industries (SEV) and

2010, the value of Hellenic exports to Azerbaijan reached 9,230,000 USD and the
value of Azerbaijani exports to Greece reached 120,258 USD.
